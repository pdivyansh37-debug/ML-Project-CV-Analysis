# Code of Conduct

At **TonyCV**, we are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, and inclusive environment for contributors, users, and collaborators. Our community thrives on respect, collaboration, and innovation, and we expect all participants to uphold these values.

## Core Principles
- **Respect & Inclusivity**: Treat all individuals with dignity, regardless of gender, identity, race, ethnicity, religion, disability, or background.
- **Professionalism**: Maintain constructive communication. Disagreements are natural, but personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory remarks are not tolerated.
- **Collaboration**: Encourage knowledge sharing, mentorship, and teamwork. Contributions should aim to uplift the community and improve the project.
- **Integrity**: Ensure honesty in contributions. Plagiarism, misrepresentation of work, or falsifying credentials is strictly prohibited.
- **Safety**: Provide a harassment-free experience for everyone. Offensive comments, trolling, or disruptive behavior will result in corrective action.

## Expected Behavior
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Provide constructive feedback and accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and project.
- Show empathy towards other community members.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Sexualized language or imagery.
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Any conduct that disrupts community harmony.

## Enforcement
- Reports of misconduct will be reviewed by the maintainers.
- Consequences may include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the community.
- All enforcement will be fair, transparent, and consistent with the GSSoC Covenant Code of Conduct.

## Attribution
This Code of Conduct is adapted from the **Contributor Covenant** and aligned with **GSSoC guidelines**, ensuring TonyCV remains a safe, collaborative, and innovative space for all contributors.
